Using Pix

We've been trying out Pix at the HATCH makerspace in Watertown to make it easier for people to share what they're working on. We use Pix along with a whiteboard for people to post images and annotations of their projects:

To help people get started, I created this small sign that is posted right next to Pix:

Pix signage [ PDF | PNG | AI ]

I recommend taping a white backdrop (14.5"x11") on the table underneath Pix. Optionally, you can use a small clip lamp to provide additional lighting:

The current Pix setup at HATCH

Feel free to extend Pix in any and all ways. If you do something new with Pix, please share with #madewithpix!

If you run into any problems, please report the issue.

Happy documenting!
